About

Concept

Codeplay is a place where every moments started with a cup of coffee and pastries. Codeplay has through so many moments that's shared by you, and this is why we believe in the power of a small bean and improving our cafe to be the best in serving you happy and best vibes for everybody to connects with others in the middle of urban living.

History

Codeplay has taken the initiative to transform a small home from this little street into a creative, contemporary cafe. Style is a mix of simple and new, quality and craft. For urban people with love and conviction. This same place has been transformed with care and respect for environment, for the people to gather into a community.

Vision

Codeplay actively promotes emerging artists and develops a whole range of creative activities in the broadest sense. In our commitment to the artists, we grant the scope to experiment, which opens new routes for development. The opportunities for development are not limited to the art platform and quarterly Cwartaal. In addition, lectures, artist talks and other artistic activities will be taking place. Visitors are stimulated to explore the artistic in a rapidly changing world.
